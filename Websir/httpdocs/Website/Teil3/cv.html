On this page, you will be informed about my educational and non-educational path.


I am currently training as an application developer EFZ with a vocational baccalaureate in business at the IMS Bern. This training enables me to acquire knowledge in software development and at the same time integrate economic aspects. Before that, I attended secondary school in Vechigen, primary schools in Utzigen and Littewil and kindergarten in Utzigen. In order to gain practical experience, I took part in various activity days in different companies, which gave me valuable insights into the professional world and strengthened my decision to pursue application development.

From 2013 to 2023, I was actively involved in Pfadi Mühlistei in Deiswill near Bern, where I gained a lot of valuable experience in group dynamics and teamwork. In 2014, I discovered my passion for dance at the Alfonsos Ballet School in Boll near Bern, where I developed my dancing skills. At the same time, I immersed myself in the world of figure skating by participating in the Cool Dreams of EC Burgdorf from 2015 to 2017 and training at EC Münchenbuchsee since 2022. I am also interested in the sci-fi novels, which I read with passion, in English and German.
